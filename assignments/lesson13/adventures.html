Choose your own adventure!

Beginner Course

Float along our lazy river and simply have fun taking pictures and taking in all the beatury of nature all around you. A tour guide will be with you all along the way to point out and educate all members of your part about the plants and wildlife you will see.
Length: 4 hours.
Skill: Beginner.
Cost: 50 per person.
Lunch isn't included, but there will be time to stop along the shore and have a snack so bring something yummy to eat!

Intermediate course

A slightly more fast pace trip touching on some white waters towards the end. There is very little risk of fall out, so don't worry, you can have fun even if you don't know how to swim.
Length: 4 hours.
Skill: Intermediate.
Cost: 75 per person.
Lunch isn't included, but there will be time to stop along the shore and have a snack so bring something yummy to eat!

Advanaced course

This course is hard and fast paced. Though the same length as our other rivers, the waters move faster so the trip will be shorter. This will be less about enjoying the scenery and more about enjoying the ride. There will be a trainner there with you to ensure everyones safety.
Length: 2 hours.
Skill: Advanced.
Cost: 100 per person.
Lunch is included in this package at the end of the ride as you rest by the fireplace in our own lodge (because you will be cold and wet by the end!)
